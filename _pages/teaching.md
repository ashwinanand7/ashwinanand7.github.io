<TEXT-TAG style="font-size:30px;"> Class Syllabi </TEXT-TAG>

__Introductory Proof-Writing (Math 8)__: Propositional logic, proof techniques (contrapositive, contradiction, casework), Well-Ordering Principle, Principle of Mathematical Induction, Cardinality (Cantor-Schroder-Bernstein).

__Graduate Measure Theory (Math 201A)__: Sigma algebras, outer measures, measures, Lebesgue-Stieltjes integration, convergence theorems, modes of convergence, product measures.

__Analysis III (Math 118C)__: Multivariable calculus, Fourier Analysis, Measure Theory

__Ring Theory (Math 111B)__: Rings, ideals, types of rings (UFDs, PIDs, Euclidean Domains, Bezout Domains), Zorn's lemma,  irreducibility criteria, modules.

__Data Structures & Algorithms (CS 130B)__: Greedy algorithms, dynamic programming, divide-and-conquer, solving recurrence relations, approximation algorithms, NP-completeness.
